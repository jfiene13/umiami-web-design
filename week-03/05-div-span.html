<div> and <span>

<div> and <span> are generic elements.

<div> is a block element.

<span> is an inline element.

<div> and <span> become essential for laying out a page in HTML.

Block elements

Block elements begin on a new line. They may contain other block elements or inline elements. By default, their width is equal to that of the browser window.

Examples: <div>, <h1>, <hr>, <p>

Inline elements

Inline elements do not begin on a new line. They may only contain their contents and other inline elements. They may never contain block elements.

Examples: <abbr>, <code>, <img>, <strong>
